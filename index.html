Road accidents in United Kingdom (UK)

Automobile accidents describe among the leading causes of death in developed countries. There are also many victims on the side of adolescents and young adults, especially males. Road accidents are considered an epidemic of the twentieth century because they claim the lives of many people each year. The causes are to be found in speeding, driving while intoxicated or under the influence of psychoactive substances, failure to comply with or absence of safety provisions, distraction at the wheel due to the use of mobile phones, dangerous road infrastructure and non-compliance with the Highway Code. The Kingdoms are translated as one of the safest countries in terms of road safety. They have achieved this through the combination of regulation, enforcement and education. Our data visualization project is based on road accident data in the United Kingdom between 2005 and 2016. The object of our study is to highlight the number of accident cases through two graphs, one which shows the evolution between 2005 and 2016 according to gender and the other with a catre that shows the different cities have occurred accidents.
